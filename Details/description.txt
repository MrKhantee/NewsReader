News Reader enables to listen to every single article of famous newspapers of 4 Nations, India(also available in hindi) , Australia, England and USA. No matter whether you are working at gym, walking or cooking at home, lets news reader accompany you and enrich your day. 
No more early waking, no more holding newspapers, no more collecting articles, your important news is just a click away. The effort is only to listen and not to read.

Easy understanding for our less blessed mates, unable to read and write.

An app of common people, for the common people and by the common people.
